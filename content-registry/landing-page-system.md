# Citizenship by Investment Lead-Generation Landing Page System

This document defines the conversion and SEO framework for a premium citizenship-by-investment consultancy brand under one domain.

## Shared design and conversion system

### Reusable components
- Premium hero with form or primary CTA
- Trust grid
- Process timeline
- Program comparison cards
- FAQ accordion
- Qualification form
- CTA panel
- Compliance notice block
- Social proof / trust signal strip

### Core form fields
- Full name
- Email
- Phone / WhatsApp
- Current country of residence
- Nationality
- Budget range
- Main objective
- Optional notes

### Core CTA language
- Request a private consultation
- See your best-fit route
- Review suitable programs
- Discuss your profile confidentially

### Trust signals to repeat selectively
- Confidential handling of enquiries
- Work with licensed providers only
- Structured qualification before introductions
- AML / KYC / due diligence awareness
- Realistic expectations, no guaranteed outcomes

## 1. Pillar Page

### `/citizenship-by-investment/`

#### 1. Strategy summary
- Broad authority page
- Main SEO hub and internal-linking parent
- High-intent lead generation for first-time serious enquirers

#### 2. Full landing page wireframe
- Hero with concise qualification form
- Who this is for
- Benefits and trade-offs
- How the process works
- Program overview cards
- Why clients use an advisor
- Confidential consultation CTA
- FAQ

#### 3. Full final copy

**Hero**
- Eyebrow: `Private advisory for global mobility planning`
- H1: `Citizenship by investment for investors who want a clearer, more structured route`
- Subtext: `We help qualified clients understand official citizenship by investment programs, compare realistic options, and speak with licensed providers that fit their profile.`
- CTA: `Request a private consultation`
- Support text: `Confidential, realistic, and structured from the first conversation.`

**Who this is for**
- Heading: `Who usually explores citizenship by investment`
- Copy: `Most serious applicants are not looking for a dramatic shortcut. They are usually planning for mobility, family optionality, or long-term international flexibility. The right route depends on your goals, your family structure, and how cleanly your profile can move through due diligence.`

**Benefits**
- Heading: `What a second citizenship can and cannot do`
- Copy: `A second citizenship can support global mobility, family planning, and longer-term optionality. It does not remove the need for compliance, and it is not a substitute for understanding tax, residence, or business rules in the jurisdictions that matter to you.`

**Process**
- Heading: `How the process usually works`
- Steps:
  - `Initial qualification and profile review`
  - `Shortlist of suitable official programs`
  - `Introduction to licensed provider`
  - `Due diligence, submission, and formal review`

**Programs**
- Heading: `The main Caribbean programs investors compare most often`
- Copy: `For most clients, the practical shortlist includes St. Kitts & Nevis, Dominica, Antigua & Barbuda, Saint Lucia, and Grenada. The differences are real, but usually smaller than generic ranking pages imply.`

**Advisor section**
- Heading: `Why many investors want a structured advisory layer first`
- Copy: `The value of an advisor is not just information. It is helping you avoid the wrong route, the wrong provider, and the wrong expectations before time and money are committed.`

**Final CTA**
- Heading: `Need a realistic shortlist based on your profile?`
- Copy: `Share your details and we’ll help you understand which official routes appear most relevant before you are introduced to a licensed provider.`
